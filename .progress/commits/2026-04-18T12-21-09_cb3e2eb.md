## What it does

`gray-matter`'s `Buffer` dependency was failing silently in the SSR bundle, causing only the last ~2 paragraphs of each lesson to render. Replaced with a 15-line inline YAML frontmatter parser — no deps, no `Buffer`.

## Verification

`01-mental-model` now renders 24 paragraphs + 7 `<h2>`s (full content).

## Why it matters

Lesson content is the product. Silent truncation of 90% of each lesson would have shipped a broken Bronze.
